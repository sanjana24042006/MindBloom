What is MindBloom?

MindBloom is an all-in-one mental wellness companion designed to help you:

✨ Track Your Mood: Check in with how you're feeling and explore what triggers your emotions.

📓 Journal Your Thoughts: Write freely in a safe, private space — a judgment-free zone for reflection.

🧘 Explore Wellness Activities: Discover personalized activities like meditation and breathing exercises.

🔔 Stay Consistent: Set daily reminders to check in with yourself.

All your data stays completely private and is stored securely on your account.
